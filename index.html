“ Safety is the Axiom of Progress. ”

Founded in January 2025, Protoethik is on a mission to develop AI systems that think deeply, reason transparently, and genuinely understand the world. We believe AI should empower humanity, not replace human judgment.

The pace of artificial intelligence development often outstrips our ability to fully comprehend the mechanisms at play. At Protoethik, we intentionally slow down the deployment phase to accelerate the understanding phase.

Our first product, TyloAI, demonstrates what's possible when cognitive architecture meets responsible development. With the ode-7 model family, we're not just building tools—we're crafting thinking partners that adapt to your needs and grow with you.
